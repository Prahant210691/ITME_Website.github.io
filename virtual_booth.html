Climate :

The climate in Greater Noida during December can be summarized as mild and very dry. In December, the winter in Greater Noida and is typically the 2nd coldest month of the year. Daytime maximum temperatures average around 23°C (73°F), whilst at night 8°C (47°F) is normal.

How to Reach

IEML is located in Greater Noida, within easy reach from the Indira Gandhi International Airport, Delhi on the new 8-lane Greater Noida Expressway. The venue is easily accessible by road from central Delhi Airport via NH 24 within 45 mins.

There are several routes available from Delhi.

Route 1 : Via Ring Road New Delhi

Take the DND (Noida Toll) bridge and approach Greater Noida exit after the toll booths. Continue past Film City and onto the Expressway.

Route 2 : Via Mathura Road - Apollo Hospital - Kalindi Kunj

Drive past the Apollo Hospital & take left for Kalindi Kunj to reach Amity flyover & take loop to get on the Expressway.

Route 3 : Via Ghaziabad - NH24 - Mayur Vihar

Take NH 24 and continue left from Akshardham Setu on Mayur Vihar - Noida road, through the DND underpass. Continue past Film City and onto the Expressway.

Via Metro Route from International Airport

International Airport T2 to Airport Metro Station - Airport Metro Station to New Delhi - New Delhi to Rajiv Chowk - Rajiv Chowk to Noida Sector 52 - Noida Sector 52 to Noida Sector 51 - Noida Sector 51 to Knowledge Park

The nearest metro station to India Expo Mart is Knowledge Park II metro station.
